MobiSession Moving your sessions since 2015

Welcome to MobiSession. This demo is an experiment with transferring sessions between different devices.

Basic usage:

  1. 1. Visit this page on device A (new session is created).
  2. 2. Update the session data by entering something into the textbox and pressing 'Update session'.
  3. 3. Visit the page on another device (device B). If the device can read QR-codes, feel free to do it. Otherwise visit /session/{{ session['sid'] }}. Change the data and click the 'Update session'.
  4. 4. Refresh the browser on device A. The session has been changed.
